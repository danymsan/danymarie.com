A bit about me

I've been in love with design since I got my hands on my first computer making websites on Xanga and GeoCities. Remember those? The web has changed a lot since then (thank goodness). I graduated from Arizona State University with a BA in Visual Communication Design. Since then, I've helped companies design digital software and apps from healthcare software, e-comm, agriculture to social media and currently adtech for over seven years.


That work-life balance

When I'm not obsessing over details and micro interactions, I'll most likely be at a coffee shop people watching, walking around the city, catching up with friends over brunch, hiking or hunting for a great craft cocktail. Happy hours also make me very happy, but I feel like it's a given for everyone.
